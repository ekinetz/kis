The dawn of digital discovery

It is our pleasure to bring you the first issue of the KIS Beacon, a new, monthly electronic newsletter written for users of the Keyword Intelligence System (KIS) platform. In each issue, you can expect to find news, insights, tips and tricks that will help you integrate KIS into your digital content work. Thank you for being part of our community. We look forward to working with you.

NWOM —
See you at the summits

From building data-driven cohorts to optimizing the performance of your campaigns, the New Work of Marketing demands that data fuel the decisions you make. At many of the steps, the Keyword Intelligence System is your source of not only keyword data, but insights you need:

  • Inform your cohort development
  • Map your campaign across the buy cycle
  • Craft your value proposition
  • Understand your competitive landscape
  • Track your campaign’s performance

To help you unlock the potential of KIS, we’re participating in the NWoM Summit series. Our most recent stop was the Commerce Summit in Atlanta and we plan to support the upcoming Mobile/Social Summit in Boston and the Systems Summit in Austin. Look for us in the Demo Zone.

If you can’t make one of these Summits or just can’t wait to get started, reach out! Email our chief trainer, Ty Kelly and we’ll make it happen.

Marketer Workspace — Insights Direct to You

Did you know that you can access KIS insights directly from the Marketer Workspace? There is a direct link to KIS in the Apps drop down so you can get to your projects and, more importantly, your insights faster and more easily than ever.

This is only the beginning of the integration of KIS and Marketer Workspace. We’re working on an API between the two platforms that will pipe the insights you need directly to you at the step you need them. Watch this space for updates on new developments.
